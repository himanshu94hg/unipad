Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does the transaction tax work?

    UniPad has a 10% transaction tax. check our tokenomics section

  • What makes UniPad different from other coins?

    We think our strong points are our transparency, strong work ethic and commitment to push our boundaries as much and as often as possible. And of course, as a UniPad holder, we basically pay you to hold the token.

  • How can I make sure I don't get scammed by clone projects?

    Always use ONLY our official Telegram channel. Our team will NEVER DM you to ask about anything. Be EXTREMELY suspicious about anyone DM-ing you with an offer to help, airdrop or generally asking you personal information such as your wallet seed phrase. NEVER give out such information, block and report those persons immediately.

  • When trading CEXes do transaction taxes apply?

    No, transaction taxes do not apply on CEXes, they don't support it.

  • When sending UniPad to CEXes do transaction taxes apply?

    Yes, any transfer incurs the 10% tax. This also applies when you send funds to a CEX or any other wallet.
